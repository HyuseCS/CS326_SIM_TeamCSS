# Privacy Note

*Note: This document serves as a privacy notice for our application, even in its demonstration/academic phase.*

## 1. Data Collected
We collect the minimum amount of data necessary to provide our services:
- **Account Information**: Usernames, email addresses, and encrypted/hashed passwords provided during registration.
- **Usage Data**: Basic interaction data, such as login timestamps and app navigation, which helps us improve the user experience.
- **User-Generated Content**: Any profile details or messages sent through the platform.

## 2. Why We Collect This Data
- **Authentication & Security**: To verify user identity, secure accounts, and prevent unauthorized access.
- **Service Provision**: To allow users to interact with the features of the application, such as messaging and profile customization.
- **Improvement & Debugging**: To identify bugs, monitor platform health, and enhance application performance.

## 3. Data Retention
- User data is retained only for as long as the user's account remains active.
- If an account is deleted by the user, all associated personal data and identifiable information are permanently removed from our active databases within a standard operational timeframe (e.g., 30 days).
- Backups containing user data are securely overwritten on a rolling basis.

## 4. User Rights
Even as a demonstration project, we respect user rights regarding their data:
- **Right to Access**: Users can view the personal data they have provided.
- **Right to Rectification**: Users can update or correct their account information.
- **Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")**: Users can request the deletion of their account and associated personal data.
- **Right to Opt-Out**: Users can choose not to provide certain optional information, though this may limit functionality.
